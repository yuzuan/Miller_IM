# Figure 4 legend

**Figure 4 | Paired tumor proteomes support elevation of the measurable Miller-IM protein program at recurrence.**

**A,** Recurrent-minus-primary Miller-IM protein scores in PDC000514. Eleven of 20 proteins were measurable; 72/105 patients had higher scores at recurrence (mean change 0.253, 95% CI 0.141–0.364; paired sign-flip *P* = 4.0 × 10−5). **B,** Paired changes for the 11 proteins; *RHOB, GSTM3, FOLR2, PDK4,* and *CTTNBP2* met full-proteome FDR < 0.05. **C,** Miller-IM proteins within the ranked proteome. Single-set competitive enrichment was positive in the unique-peptide analysis (NES 1.53; nominal *P* = 0.049), whereas the all-peptide sensitivity analysis was nonsignificant. PDC000514 is whole-tumor TMT and lacks IDH metadata; it cannot identify the cellular source.
